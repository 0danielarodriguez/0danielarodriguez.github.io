Outreach

Co-founder and general organizer of the Ada Lovelace Workshops, aimed at girls aged 10 to 12, with the goal of promoting STEM careers and encouraging them to continue their studies in data science, computer science, engineering, and mathematics, while also helping them understand the importance of women’s roles in science. The initiative started in 2022 with 5 venues in Argentina, and today it is present in 10 countries across Latin America with more than 80 venues. The workshops were supported by the Elsevier Mathematical Sciences Sponsorship Fund Elsevier Mathematical Sciences Sponsorship Fund and Foundation Compositio Mathematica.
